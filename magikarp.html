General Information

"Magikarp is a medium-sized fish Pokemon with large, heavy reddish-orange scales. It has large, vacant eyes and pink lips. Its pectoral and tail fins are white, but it has a stiff, three-peaked fin on its back and stomach that are both yellow. It also has long barbels. The barbels are white on a female and tan on a male."

- Courtesy of Bulbapedia

Magikarps are water pokemon that are often found with Seakings and Goldeens. They can level up to become gyarados and then mega gyarados using gyaradosite.

BEWARE: At level 20, Magikarp evolves into Gyarados. In order to preserve the Magikarp population, please avoid battling with your Magikarp.

Powers/Strengths

Magikarps are fearsome creatures. They have the following moves:

  1. Splash
  2. Tackle
  3. Flail

With the aforementioned skills. They can splash away any enemy in their wake. Just imagine. Thousands of Magikarps splashing and flailing around you. Their fearsome powers only grows with time.

Trivia

"Magikarp is based on the Asian carp, which are easily frightened by disturbances in water and can jump 8-10 feet (2.5-3 m) in the air."
"Magikarp's evolution into Gyarados is based on a legend about how carp that leapt over the Dragon Gate would become dragons. Several waterfalls and cataracts in China are believed to be the location of the Dragon Gate. This legend is an allegory of the drive and efforts needed to overcome obstacles. This may have inspired an element in Pokemon Snap where, after certain steps are taken, a Magikarp will splash its way into a waterfall where it evolves."

-Courtesy of Bulbapedia
